📝Reconstitution Steps

  1. Wipe vial stopper with a fresh alcohol swab and allow to dry.
  2. Draw 2 mL of bacteriostatic water into syringe.
  3. Inject slowly along the vial wall — do not spray directly onto powder.
  4. Gently swirl until solution is clear. Do not shake.
  5. Label vial with reconstitution date.
  6. Store refrigerated at 2–8 °C. Use within 28 days.

📝Reconstitution Steps
